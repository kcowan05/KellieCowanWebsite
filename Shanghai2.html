Shanghai

Shanghai

China's biggest city, Shanghai is unlike anywhere else in the People's Republic, home to some of the world's tallest buildings and biggest corporations. Despite being China's most outwardly modern city, there is history at every turn, from the ancient Jing'an Temple to the vast People's Square.



The Highlights

Old Meets New

This was something that surprised me about Shanghai - The old temples surrounded by modern architecture and skyscrapers, but somehow it still remained beautiful.

Jing'An Temple

Jing'An Temple is still in use by Monks, the stature of Buddah was the main feature of the temple where the public came to pray and burn insence.

The Bund At Night

My favourite night of the whole trip. The Bund of Shanghai is just fantastic at night when it is all lit up, such a vibrant place full of nightlife and tourists from all over the world.

Kellie Cowan 2018
