The Yellow Mountains

One of the highlights of the trip, has to be the Yellow Mountains of Huangshan. The mountain range features “Four Wonders” - beautifully-shaped pines trees, fantastic rock peaks, sea of clouds and hot springs.



The Highlights

Cable Car Down

A moment of relief when we discovered the cable car that would bring us to the bottom of the mountain, after 7 hours of hiking up! Beautiful views of deep into the valley of the mountain.

View From The Top

As you can see, the view form the top was absolutely incredible. After hours trying to get the best photo location, we finally settles on this bench.
